Downloading TEAMS

In order to run the TEAMS application from within GREET, download the two DLL files from this link:

  • https://github.com/RIT-LECDM/TEAMS-Module/releases/tag/v1.0

In order to install the application, you must copy the two DLL files to GREET's application folder, or navigate to "My Documents" -> "Greet" -> "Plugins" and place them there.

Welcome to TEAMS-Module

The Total Energy & Emissions Analysis for Maritime Systems Model, TEAMS, was developed by undergraduate researchers at the Rochester Institute of Technology with contributions from the University of Delaware. TEAMS expands on the functionality of GREET by allowing for the life cycle fuel analysis of maritime vessels. More information on TEAMS is available here. The TEAMS Module runs within GREET 2013, and uses GREETâ€™s fuel pathways as part of its calculations.

Authors and Contributors

The TEAMS plugin expands on work done by the Rochester Institute of Technology and the University of Delaware, bringing the original spreadsheet layout of TEAMS into GREET 2013 with an easy to use interface. The plugin was developed by Rob Clifford (@Grub0) and Shayde Nofziger (@ShaydeNofziger). The project was headed by Dr. James Winebrake.
